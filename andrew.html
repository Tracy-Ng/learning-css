Rachel Andrew

She is Editor in Chief of Smashing Magazine, a Member of The CSS Working Group, and the W3C representative for Fronteers. She is Managing Director and founder of web development company edgeofmyseat.com. Founded in 2001 as a web development consultancy, the company moved into products launching Perch CMS in 2009 and Notist in 2018. Her professional web development skills range from front and back-end development to operations. Andrew is also an experienced writer and editor focusing on technical and business writing.
